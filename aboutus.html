How to play the game?
Players can pay Mushroom Coins to upgrade themselves to the higher level

During the game, players can use their DXN to buy more Power Up Weapons
A.Get as much Mushroom as possible in order to beat the other player
B.The winning player gets to split the 50% of the total DXN prize pool
C.Each round must have 1 banker & 2 players
D.The duration of one game is about 500 blocks, which is about 10 minutes
E.The banker must at least put 100 USD Equivalent DXN in each game
F.The banker takes 50% of the total DXN prize pool
G.We take 1% of the banker's share in each game as company profit
